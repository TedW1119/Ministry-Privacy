Ministry Support

Church scheduling assistant for iOS

Ministry helps church volunteers view upcoming service schedules, manage personal duty reminders, and track multiple members' schedules in one place.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add family or team members?
Go to the Settings tab (gear icon) and use the Members section to add names. Enter each name exactly as it appears on the church schedule. You can add up to 7 members.
Why don't I see any schedules?
Make sure you've added at least one member whose name matches the church schedule. Names must match exactly (e.g. "John D." including the period). Pull down on the Home screen to refresh.
How do notifications work?
Ministry uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming duties and schedules. You can choose reminder timings (2 hours, 6 hours, 1 day, etc.) in Settings. No notification data is sent to external servers.
Can I edit a schedule?
Yes. Tap the pencil icon on a service card in the Calendar view to edit role assignments. Edits are stored locally on your device and shown with a gold "Edited" indicator. You can revert changes at any time.
How do I create a personal duty reminder?
Tap the "+" button on the Home screen. Fill in the category, title, date, and time, then save. You'll receive notifications based on your chosen reminder timings.
How do I delete my data?
All personal data (members, duties, preferences) is stored on your device. Deleting the app removes all local data. Bug reports and anonymous logs on our server are automatically deleted after 90 days.
What does the app collect?
Ministry does not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or location data. It fetches schedules and events from our server (read-only) and can send bug reports and anonymous diagnostics. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Report a Bug

Found something wrong? Use the "Report a Bug" option in the app's Settings tab. Include a description of what happened and we'll look into it.
